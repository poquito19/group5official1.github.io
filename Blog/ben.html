Angry Birds is a Finnish action, puzzle, and strategy based media franchise created by Rovio Entertainment. The game series focuses on the eponymous flock of colorful angry birds who try to save their eggs from green-colored pigs.

Ghost Pig

Ghost Pig is an unreleased plush made by Commonwealth for the Halloween plush set. The plush would've been released sometime in 2011 as part of the set. It was never released and was most likely replaced by Pumpkin Hat Pig when the set released, as two pigs were not needed. This plush was acquired by Angry Birds plush YouTuber Maximus Overdrive in January 2023.
